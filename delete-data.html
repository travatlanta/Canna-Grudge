User Data Deletion

Last updated October 23, 2025

Use this page to request deletion of your CannaGrudge account and associated data.

What gets deleted

  • Your authentication profile (name, email, UID) stored via Firebase Authentication.
  • Any profile data we store that is tied to your UID (if applicable).

How to request deletion

  1. Open your dashboard while signed in and copy your User ID (UID) if shown; if not, just use the email on your account.
  2. Send us an email from the same address you use to log in to the Service, with the subject Delete My Data.
  3. Include your name, the email on the account, and your UID (if available).

Verification & timeline

We may ask you to verify your identity. Once verified, we aim to complete deletion within 30 days unless a longer period is required by law or to prevent fraud/abuse.
