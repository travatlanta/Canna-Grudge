Fighter Guidelines & Weight Classes

Welcome to Nirvana x Diablo first annual Canna Grudge. This is meant to be fun and entertaining for all of us. This is NOT an official boxing match or exhibition match. It is simply a couple brands getting together and sparring in a ring for entertainment purposes.

Bouts will be wearing head gear, sparring with 16oz gloves and mouth guards just to be safe.

  • 3 rounds, 1.5 min each
  • Proof of physical within 30 days of event
  • Active FA card linked to representing facility
  • Emergency medical staff on site
  • No prior combat sports experience required
  • Same weight class opponents only
